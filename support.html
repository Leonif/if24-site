Support

Questions, problems or ideas about IF24 - you're in the right place.

Contact the developer

IF24 is made by an independent developer, and email is answered personally - usually within 1-2 days. Write to leonif1@gmail.com. For bug reports, a short description of what happened plus a screenshot helps a lot.

Is IF24 free?

Yes. IF24 is free on the App Store and the fasting timer works without a subscription or sign-up.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no sign-up, and your fasting data stays on your device.

What fasting schedules does IF24 support?

16:8, 18:6, 20:4 and custom intermittent fasting schedules. The eating window length is derived from your plan automatically.

How do streaks work?

A day counts toward your streak when you reach your fasting goal that day. Miss a day and the streak resets - milestones at 3, 7, 14 and 30 days.

My timer looks wrong after I forgot to stop a fast

Use the Last meal control on the start screen - the app back-dates your fast to the time you actually stopped eating, so the timer stays accurate.

Are the metabolic phases medical data?

No. IF24 shows estimated fat burning, ketosis and autophagy phases based on typical fasting timing. It does not measure your body and is not medical advice. See also our privacy policy and terms of use.
