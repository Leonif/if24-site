Questions

Good to know

Is IF24 free?

Yes. IF24 is free to download and use, with no account and no subscription.

Does IF24 measure ketosis or autophagy?

No. IF24 doesn't measure anything in your body. The phases it shows are estimated, based on typical fasting timing, and are for general information only.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up. Your fasting history stays on your device.

Which fasting schedules are supported?

Common time-restricted schedules like 16:8, 18:6 and 20:4, plus custom windows and the Last Meal start.
