A free alternative to Fastic

IF24 is a free iOS intermittent fasting timer — no account, no data collected, and live metabolic phases at no cost.

Fastic is a feature-rich fasting and nutrition app, but much of it sits behind the Fastic Plus subscription and it requires creating an account. If you want a focused fasting timer that's completely free and skips the sign-up, IF24 is a simple alternative.

Why pick IF24

If you mainly want a free, private fasting timer that clearly shows fat burning, ketosis and autophagy as your fast progresses, IF24 does that without a subscription or account. Fastic is worth it if you want its broader nutrition and coaching ecosystem.
