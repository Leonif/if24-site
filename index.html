Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about IF24.

Is IF24 free?

Yes. IF24 is free on the App Store and the fasting timer works without a subscription or sign-up.

What is IF24?

IF24 is a free iOS intermittent fasting timer that tracks your fasting window and shows estimated metabolic phases — fat burning, ketosis and autophagy — based on typical fasting timing.

Does IF24 show autophagy and ketosis phases?

IF24 shows estimated fat burning, ketosis and autophagy phases based on typical fasting timing. Actual timing varies from person to person, so this is general educational information, not medical advice.

What fasting schedules does IF24 support?

IF24 supports 16:8, 18:6, 20:4 and custom intermittent fasting schedules.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no sign-up, and the App Store privacy label declares that the app does not collect any data.

Which devices does IF24 run on?

IF24 runs on iPhone (iOS) and is available worldwide on the App Store.

Support Privacy & Support App Store

IF24 provides general information about intermittent fasting and shows estimated metabolic phases based on typical timing — it does not measure your body and is not medical advice. Fasting may not be suitable for everyone; consult a healthcare professional before starting any fasting routine.
